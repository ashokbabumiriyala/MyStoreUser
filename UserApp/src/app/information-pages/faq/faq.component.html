YOU CAN FIND THE ANSWERS TO MOST OF YOUR QUESTIONS HERE. IF YOU HAVE A SPECIAL QUERY PLEASE EMAIL US AT SUPPORT@MY3.STORE . OUR TEAM WILL RESPOND TO YOU IN 1-4 BUSINESS DAYS.

1.Can I place my orders even when stores are closed?

Ans: Yes, you can & the stores will send you the notification of designated pick up time or will let you know the delivery schedule.

2.Can I pay online for all my purchases?

Ans: We are currently working on an online payment portal. Currently it is COD.

3.What is My3Kart doing to keep customers and employees safe?

Ans: At My3Kart employee and customer safety is given top priority. We ensure all our delivery executives and employees follow the WHO defined safety protocols.

4. Is it safe to receive orders?

Ans: For guidance on COVID-19, please refer to the World Health Organization website

5. Are you delivering essential products only?

Ans: As per the government guidelines we are delivering essential and non-essential products that are in our catalogues of products.

6. What is the pick-up option?

Ans: Once your order is ready, the retailer store will keep packaged and ready for you to pick it up at a time of your convenience.

7. How to collect your order?

Ans: When the package is ready for pickup, you'll receive a notification on the app informing the same. Details of the store address and its working hours will also be shared in these notifications.

At the store location, present a valid photo ID for the purpose of verification. Any of the photo IDs mentioned below can be presented at the time of picking up the order.

• Voter ID card

• PAN Card

• Passport

• Driving License

• Aadhaar Card

• Employer ID card with photo

• ID card provided by institutions such as Student ID card with photo

In case you aren't able to make it to the store, you can send your friend to collect your package. Your friend can collect your package on presenting a copy of your valid photo ID, duly signed by you.

You'll have 48 hours to pick up your package after it is delivered to the store. If your package is not picked up in 48 hours, it will be returned for a refund if already paid in advance.

8. What can I return?

Ans: Items are returnable if you've received them in a condition that is physically damaged, has missing parts or accessories, defective or different from its description on the product detail page on My3Kart.
